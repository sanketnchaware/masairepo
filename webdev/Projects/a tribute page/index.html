Lekhraj Kripalani

(15 December 1876 – 18 January 1969)

The founder of the Brahma Kumaris.

Also Known as Bramha Baba, Dada Lekhraj.

⭐The important dates in their life:-

  • In 1936, Lekhraj established a spiritual organisation called Om Mandali.
  • In 1937, Lekhraj named some of the members of his satsang as a managing committee, and transferred his fortune to the committee.
  • On 18 January 1939, the mothers of two girls aged 12 and 13 filed an application against Om Mandali, in the Court of the Additional Magistrate in Karachi.
  • After the partition of India, the Brahma Kumaris moved to Mount Abu (Rajasthan) in India in April 1950.
  • Lekhraj died on 18 January 1969, and the Brahma Kumaris subsequently expanded to other countries.
