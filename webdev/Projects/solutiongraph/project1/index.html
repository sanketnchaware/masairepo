TRICOLOGY AND HAIR TRANSPLANT

Tricology focuses on the conditions and problems related to hair and scalp and also the treatment for those problems. At Vtiara we have an expert tricologist and hair transplant surgeon Dr Madan Kumar B J who is specialised in diagnosing and treating hair related problems like hairloss, baldness, hair thinning, dandruff, oily scalp etc. At Vtiara we have treatments like PRP, Mesotherapy and low level light therapy for hairloss which helps in reducing hairloss and also thickens your existing hair follicles. For baldness we have treatments like Hair Transplantation in which hair follicles will be extracted from the backside of your scalp and it will be implanted in the bald area. In few cases, the donor area(backside of scalp) will be weak. In that case we will either extract hairs from body parts like beard, chest and thighs. We are also specialised in performing beard transplant, eyebrow transplant, moustache transplant, female hair transplant etc with advanced techniques like non-shave method, DHI method and FUE method. SMP is nothing but scalp tattooing through which we create an illusion of hair follicles. This micropigmentation is also used for permanent makeup like eyebrow thickening, permanent lip color etc. We at Vtiara use imported inks from German and UK which are safe and natural.
